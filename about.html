About Mineral Wisdom

"It is where it was in the past"

Mineral Wisdom is a human development organization formed by the CSR Departement of PT. Green Mineral Mining in 1990. Mr. Lery Susanto was elected as the chief. During its first year, it has gain high reputation from the locals. It is famous for its dedication to ensuring high quality education service is gained by school children in the province. Mineral Wisdom alocates 70% of its capital source to educational development. Whlie the other 30% is provided for health, food, and shelters. Any parties who may have interest in cooperating with our programs are welcomed. Should you intend to have a discussion on our program, please fill the form.
